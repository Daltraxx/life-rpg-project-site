About LifeRPG

LifeRPG is all about taking the aspects that make progressing and levelling in a traditional RPG so addictive and satisfying and applying them to life itself.

By gamifying life and the pursuit of one's goals, we hope to make the practice of establishing productive habits and eliminating harmful ones as fun and immediately gratifying as progressing a character from a level 1 amateur to overpowered magnificence.
